Carmack made many hugely influential games, establishing the entire First Person Shooter genre. Some of his games include:

  • Doom
  • Wolfenstein 3D
  • Commander Keen

Most recently, he has worked with Oculus, a company building a Virtual Reality headset, to help usher gaming into the next era of perspective like he did with the original first-person games. In addition to his massive expertise in game and graphics programming, John Carmack has taken a deep interest in Aerospace and Rocket Science, spending much of his time and money working with rocketry groups in the Mojave Desert.
